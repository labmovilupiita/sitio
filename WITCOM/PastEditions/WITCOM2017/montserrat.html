Speakers

Cancún, Mexico

Montserrat Muciño Barquín

Title: Women and digital: a world of opportunities

Womenwill: Women Entrepreneurs

When women thrive, we all thrive. Empowering women through access to digital information, technology and tools allows them not only to grow and develop, but to change the world around them. Let's help close gender gaps and boost the economy in Mexico.

Born in Cdmx, graduated from Universidad TecMilenio Cancún in the Degree of Graphic Design and Animation, and ISDI with the Master in Internet Business. With a digital strategy approach in both social networks and advertising media, she has worked in companies such as Universidad TecMilenio, Interlingua and Google México.

She's currently working as a Google Marketing Consultant.
