Dr Cesar Viho (IRISA Francia)
On Quality of Experience (QoE)-based resource management in wireless networks		 Classical methods of managing wireless networks are based on technical parameters such as delays, losses, jitter, and etc. These parameters are also used to evaluate the Quality of Service (QoS). The issue there is that this QoS evaluation may not correlate with the users’ experience so called Quality of Experience (QoE). In this talk, we will present the state of the art and benefits in using this new paradigm QoE for resource management in heterogeneous wireless networks and for the evaluation of multimedia applications.
