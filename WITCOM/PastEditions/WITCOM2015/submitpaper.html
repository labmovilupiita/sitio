Working Language

All papers must be written in English and should be sent for review in PDF, no more than 10 pages including illustrations, results and references Submissions must be original and must have not been published previously or be under consideration for publication while being evaluated for these congress. You should not shorten the camera-ready version in comparison with the version submitted for review, unless the reviewer required this; in any case such shortening would not reduce the additional fee. Speeches shall be presented in English. This is not mandatory if the entire audience is fluent in another language, in which case the latter language can be used.

Format

Please provide your paper in the form in which it should appear in the book (but without page numbers, running heads, and copyright note). However, since the review process is double blind, please do not indicate the author names or any information that may disclose the authors identity (obviously, in the camera-ready version you should indicate the names). The papers should be prepared following the instructions from Springer LNCS series available at:

  • Springer format

We cannot guarantee publication of any paper that does not follow these guidelines. Please do not hesitate to ask any questions. The camera-ready version of accepted papers must be submitted in an editable format(PDF+Word) or (PDF +Latex).
