This is WITCOM 2015

International Congress on Telematics, Computing and Communications

WITCOM aims bringing together researchers, developers, users, entrepreneurs and practitioners to discuss and present trend topics and novelty systems based on {telematics}, {computing} and {communications}, fostering interdisciplinary discussions and research in all aspects of Telematics.

WITCOM provides a forum for original research contributions of researchers, post graduates, PHD students, and practitioners

We offer an open space that covers theoretical, conceptual, design, and implementation aspects of telecommunications and computing, ranging from applications and models, to user interfaces, experiments and prototypes.
