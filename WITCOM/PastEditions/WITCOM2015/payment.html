Fee for registered authors (Public attendant: free of charge)

The Conference can be attended as either Public Attendant or as a Registered Author; the corresponding fees are:

  • Public attendant: FREE of charge.

  • Registered Author: MXN $1321.00.

The registration process for authors who will present their accepted papers in the Conference is made up by following steps:

  • Payment of MXN $1321.00
  • Send an electronic copy of the PAYMENT VOUCHER (with attached information) to witcom.upiita@ipn.mx

Payment should be made to:

Amount:    MXN $1321.00
Beneficiary name:    Instituto Politecnico Nacional (UPIITA)
Account Number:    0150912077
CLABE code:    012180001509120770
Bank:    BBVA Bancomer, S.A. de C.V.

Please, send an electronic copy of the PAYMENT VOUCHER to the address witcom.upiita@ipn.mx, including the following information:

  • Subject: WITCOM 2015
  • Body: Submission number (paper ID) and name of the author who is registering to present the paper.
  • In case of needing a tax invoice (factura), please clearly state this and include the corresponding tax information (cédula fiscal) in the mail.
  • In case of needing a simple invoice (recibo), please include a RFC.
  • Notice that tax invoices can only be generated if this mail is sent at most the following day after the payment is made.

Registered paper authors are invited to the event's dinner which will be held the night of Thursday the 5th at the restaurant Monte Cristo / Antiguo Mesón Mejicano. The address is: Av. Insurgentes Norte #1980, Gustavo A. Madero, Lindavista, 07300 Cuidad de Mexico, D.F.
