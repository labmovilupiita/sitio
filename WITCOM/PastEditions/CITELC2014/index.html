First International Congress on Telematics, Computing and Communications (CITELC 2014)

Conference

CITELC 2014 International Congress on Telematics, Computing and Communications is the result of talks and workshops celebrated in 2012 and 2013, where professional and industrial experiences were treated by specialists and graduates from computing, communications, and telematics field.

CITELC aims bringing together researchers, developers, users, entrepreneurs and practitioners to discuss and present trend topics and novelty systems based on telematics, computing and communications, fostering interdisciplinary discussions and research in all aspects of Telematics.

The conference provides a forum for original research contributions of researchers, post graduates, PHD students, and practitioners that covers theoretical, conceptual, design, and implementation aspects of telecommunications and computing ranging from applications, models, user interfaces experiments and prototypes. Researchers, students, entrepreneurs, professionals and practitioners are invited to submit their contributions to CITELC 2014.
